Welcome to AutoCheck Outs.

Revolutionizing the shopping experience with smart technology. Say goodbye to long queues and manual billing — experience seamless, automated checkout with AutoCheck Out.

Key-Features

For Customer

AutoCheck Out offers a seamless checkout experience by eliminating long queues with instant product detection and billing. With AI-powered item recognition and secure mobile payments, customers enjoy a fast, hassle-free, and efficient shopping journey.

For Shop Owners

Automating billing reduces staff workload and enhances operational efficiency, especially during peak hours. Smart analytics provide insights into sales trends, while error-free transactions improve customer trust and store loyalty.

Advanced AI Technology

Powered by OpenCV and Vision Transformers, our system delivers high-speed, accurate item detection adaptable to various store environments. With continuous learning and robust security, it ensures reliability and scalability for future retail needs.

How It Works?

Customers simply place items at checkout, where AI automatically detects them and generates an instant bill. Payments are securely processed via the AutoCheck Out app, allowing a smooth grab-and-go experience with no waiting time.

Cost & Energy Efficiency

By minimizing labor costs and operational expenses, AutoCheck Out helps store owners save money while enhancing efficiency. Digital receipts promote sustainability, and the system’s optimized power usage ensures eco-friendly operations.

Future Expansion & Integration

With multi-store support, loyalty program integration, and third-party payment compatibility, AutoCheck Out is designed for scalability. Stores can customize features to match their branding, making it a future-ready solution for modern retail.
